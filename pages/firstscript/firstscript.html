Computer Science:

Computer Science is the study of algorithms and processes that interact with data, that can also be expressed as data in the form of programs. Algorithms can store, manipulate and communicate information to a user. A Computer Scientist studies the theory of computation and practices designing software systems. Any program can be written in three control structures:

Sequential is the default control structure, where instructions are executed line by line in the order of which they appear. Selection contains three main types of statements which are "if", "if/else" and "switch". Both "if" and "if/else" statements can be nested. Switch statements are used when there are multiple cases to choose from. Lastly, Iteration also known as repitition, repeatedly executes a series of statements while a condition is true. "While", "do/while", and "for" loops are the three main types of iteration. A loop can be either controlled by an event, or by a counter.
