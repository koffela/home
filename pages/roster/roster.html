Activities Summer 2020

Our activity staff is top notch. Before you know it, you'll be having a great time! The activities this Summer 2020 include:

Introduction to Computer Science which will teach you how to begin your journey as a programmer by introducing you to classic programs such as "Hello World" and "Guess My Number".

Beginner's Physics which will cover Kinematics (the study of motion without regard to its causes), and Dynamics (study of motion and forces that affect it).

Finally, we have Introductory Chemistry which will work with students both in and out of the lab on subjects such as Matter, Bonding, Energy, and Reactions.

For our younger students, we have our award-winning Little Science which is a class which works with kids 7-12 and teaches them about the different states of Matter, The Earth, Light, Cells, and Space.
